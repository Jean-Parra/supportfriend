First 3D Printed Prosthesis Implant

In a revolutionary breakthrough, the first successful implant of a 3D printed prosthesis was achieved. This technological milestone marks the beginning of a new era in the manufacture of customized prostheses, offering greater precision and comfort for users.

Development of Mind-Controlled Prosthetics

Thanks to advances in neuroscience and technology, prostheses controlled directly by the mind have been developed. Using brain-computer interfaces, people can control their prosthesis with the power of their thoughts, opening up new possibilities for mobility and independence.

Paralympic Games and Advances in Sports Prosthetics

The Paralympic Games have been an important stage for showcasing advances in sports prosthetics. Athletes with disabilities use specially designed prostheses that allow them to compete at the highest level in various disciplines, demonstrating human potential and self-improvement.
